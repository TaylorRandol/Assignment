Steps to Hide and Seek

  1. Pick someone to be the "Seeker"
  2. Everyone Else runs and hides
  3. The Seeker starts counting to 50
  4. When the Seeker counts to 50, they begin to look for the "Hiders"
  5. The Seeker tries their best to find everyone
  6. Everyone wins!
